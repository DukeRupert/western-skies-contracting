## Voice & Tone Guidelines

Before writing a single word of UI copy, the coding agent should understand the voice:

- **Short sentences.** If a sentence runs past 20 words, it probably needs to be two.
- **No marketing language.** Words like "top-notch," "solutions," "seamless," and "leverage" do not exist here.
- **Earn trust through specificity.** "800 feet of fencing in two days" is better than "fast and reliable."
- **Faith is present but not performative.** One scripture reference per page maximum. It's woven in, not plastered.
- **First person plural.** "We build." Not "Our team of experts delivers."
- **Montana is context, not decoration.** Reference it when it matters — snow loads, wind, local suppliers — not as atmosphere.
